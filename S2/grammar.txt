### Non-terminals ###

<program>   ::= <block>

<block>     ::= <command> <block> | <reps> <block> | <comment> <block> | ""
            
<command>   ::= <forw> <decimal> <period> | <back> <decimal> <period> | <left> <decimal> <period> 
                | <right> <decimal> <period> | <down> <period> | <up> <period> 
                | <color> <hex> <period>

<reps>      ::= <rep> <decimal> <quote> <block> <quote>
